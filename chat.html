Chaat

Papri chaat (or papdi chaat) is crispy fried-dough wafers served with typical chaat ingredients such as chickpeas, boiled potatoes, yogurt sauce, and tamarind and coriander chutneys; it may also contain pomegranate seeds.

We have to check the ingredients first:-

  • 13 papdi
  • 3 tbsp potato / aloo (boiled & chopped)
  • 3 tbsp chickpea (boiled)
  • 3 tbsp onion (finely chopped)
  • 5 tbsp curd (whisked)
  • 3 tsp green chutney
  • 3 tsp tamarind chutney
  • pinch chilli powder
  • pinch cumin powder
  • pinch chaat masala
  • pinch salt
  • 1 tbsp tomato (finely chopped)
  • 3 tbsp sev
  • 1 tsp coriander (finely chopped)

Now how to make chaat papdi

INSTRUCTIONS
  1. firstly, in a serving plate take 13 papdi. to prepare homemade papdi using wheat flour check out my papdi recipe.
  2. top with 3 tbsp potato and 3 tbsp chickpea. also, sprinkle 2 tbsp of chopped onions.
  3. now take 3 tbsp of whisked curd and drizzle over it.
  4. further, add 2 tsp of green chutney and 2 tsp of tamarind chutney.
  5. sprinkle chilli powder, cumin powder, chaat masala and salt.
  6. additionally, add 1 tbsp onion and 1 tbsp tomato.
  7. now add 1 tbsp of more curd, 1 tsp green chutney and 1 tsp tamarind chutney.
  8. top with 3 tbsp sev and garnish with 1 tsp coriander.
  9. finally, enjoy dahi papdi chaat with a hot cup of tea.
