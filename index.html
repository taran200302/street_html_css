Street food

Street foods are ready-to-eat foods and beverages prepared and/or sold by vendors or hawkers especially in the streets and other similar places.
They represent a significant part of urban food consumption for millions of low-and-middle-income consumers, in urban areas on a daily basis.
But due to covid-19 we cant eat street food so we can make street food at home to fulfil our desires.

Following are some street foods(with their reciepes) which can we make easily at home at the time of lockdown:-

  1. Gol gappa
  2. Chaat
  3. Samosa
  4. Pav Bhaji
